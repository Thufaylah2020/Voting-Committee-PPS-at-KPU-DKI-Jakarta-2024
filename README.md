# Voting-Committee-PPS-at-KPU-DKI-Jakarta-2024
2024, i am a Voting Committee in General Elections Commission (KPU RI) specially at West Jakarta region in Duri Kosambi Distric.
Managed 80.000 voter list in Duri Kosambi, Cnegkareng, West Jakarta, DKI Jakata. 135 Votting General Elections Place with 945 people Voting Organizing Group. All data in this project were obtained from the work of Voter Data Update Officers (PANTARLIH), who conducted direct field surveys to collect and verify voter information.
